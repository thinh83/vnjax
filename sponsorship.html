for the 2015 Lunar New Year Festival

Dear Sponsors,

On behalf of the Vietnamese Association of Jacksonville, we are asking for your support as a sponsor or exhibitor at our Annual Lunar New Year Festival 2015. The festival will be held on Sunday, February 8 , 2015 at the Jacksonville Fairgrounds in Jacksonville, FL.

For almost thirty years, the Vietnamese Association of Jacksonville has organized the Lunar New Year Festival entertaining hundreds of thousands of attendees from around the Southeastern United States, including politicians and community leaders. As the largest Vietnamese Lunar New Year event of its kind in Florida, the Lunar New Year Festival is one of the most anticipated annual events in the Vietnamese community. Highlights of festival include the Dragon Dance Ceremony, Vietnamese Food Festival, Multicultural Showcases, Vendor Marketplace and Youth Performances and various competitions.

By committing early, we will work closely with your company and promote it in our strategic marketing plan. The festival is the perfect opportunity to launch or reinforce your company’s brand. Our outreach during the marketing campaign will reach more than 25,000 Vietnamese in the greater Jacksonville area, and throughout Florida and we are anticipating 10,000 attending at this year’s event. Below are benefits of supporting the Lunar New Year 2015:

• Increase your company’s visibility
• Promote your company’s products and/or services
• Explore new business ventures
• Reinforce your commitment and support of the Vietnamese community
• Celebrate the Lunar New Year

On behalf of the Vietnamese Association of Jacksonville, we thank you for your consideration and look forward to celebrating with you at the 2015 Lunar New Year Celebration!

Sincerely,

Nhat Nguyen & Thinh Le
Co-Chairs of the Lunar New Year Festival

Eric Nguyen
President of Vietnamese Association of Jacksonville
